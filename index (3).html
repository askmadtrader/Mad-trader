1 Introduction

Mad Trader ("the App", "we", "our") is a personal trading compounding planner developed for individual use. This Privacy Policy describes exactly what data is collected, by whom, and how it is used.

By using Mad Trader, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2 Your Trading Data

All data you enter into Mad Trader — capital amounts, P&L, dates, notes, journal entries, and trading plans — is stored exclusively on your device using a local SQLite database.

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly trigger a cloud backup. It is never accessible to us or any third party.

3 Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (free users only): Displays banner ads on free-tier screens. Collects your device advertising ID, device model, OS version, and IP address for ad targeting. You can opt out via Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.
  • Firebase Crashlytics (all users): Collects anonymous crash logs, device model, OS version, and app version to help us fix bugs. Does NOT collect your trading data, journal text, or any personal identifiers. Governed by Google's privacy policy.
  • Google Sign-In + Google Drive (optional, opt-in only): If you sign in with Google to enable cloud backup, your trading data is stored privately in your own Google Drive account (AppData folder). This data is not accessible to us. Governed by Google's privacy policy.

4 Cloud Backup

Cloud backup via Google Drive is entirely opt-in. It is not enabled by default. Nothing is uploaded unless you tap "Sign in with Google" and then trigger a backup.

If you sign in with Google:

  • Your data is stored in a private AppData folder in your Google Drive
  • Only Mad Trader can access this folder
  • The backup is automatically deleted if you uninstall the app
  • You can sign out at any time from Settings

5 Permissions

Mad Trader explicitly blocks camera, location, contacts, and microphone access at the app level — these permissions cannot be requested by this App even if a bundled library supports them.

Permissions actually used:

  • Storage / Files: To save and open local backup JSON files, and to export journal data as Excel or PDF
  • Internet: Required for AdMob (ads), Crashlytics (crash reporting), and optional Google Drive backup. Without internet, all core planning and journalling features still work fully offline.

6 Financial Disclaimer

Mad Trader is a planning and tracking tool ONLY. It is NOT financial advice, a trading recommendation system, or a guarantee of returns.

Compound projections are mathematical simulations only. Past projections do not guarantee future results. Trading in financial markets involves significant risk. You may lose some or all of your capital. Always consult a qualified financial advisor before making trading decisions.

7 NSE Holiday Data

The App contains pre-loaded NSE (National Stock Exchange of India) holidays for 2025–2030. This data is embedded in the App and does not require an internet connection.

Always verify the official NSE calendar before trading.

8 Children's Privacy

Mad Trader is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used this App and provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

9 Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All data is visible within the App at any time
  • Export: Export everything as a JSON file via Settings → Local Backup, or your journal as Excel/PDF
  • Delete local data: Permanently delete all on-device data via Settings → Reset All Data
  • Delete cloud backup: Sign in with Google → delete backup.json from your own Drive, or simply revoke Mad Trader's access at myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Opt out of personalised ads: Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • Sign out of Google: Settings → Cloud Backup → Sign Out, which ends the Google session on this device

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the App adds new features. Updates will be reflected in new App versions. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11 Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or any other queries, email us at askmadtrader@gmail.com, or use the Contact Support / Report a Bug screens in the App (Settings → Support).
