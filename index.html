Privacy Policy Last Updated: June 2026 Mad Trader ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Mad Trader mobile application collects, uses, stores, and protects information. Information We Collect Mad Trader may store information entered by users, including: - Trading journal entries - Trade history - Trading targets - Notes and comments - App settings and preferences - Performance analytics - Other user-generated trading data This information is used solely to provide app functionality and improve the user experience. Google Sign-In If you choose to sign in with Google, Mad Trader may access limited account information such as: - Google account identifier - Email address This information is used only for authentication and cloud backup functionality. Cloud Backup and Restore When cloud backup is enabled, Mad Trader may store encrypted or serialized backup data in your personal Google Drive AppData folder. Backups may include: - Trading journals - Notes - Targets - Settings - Analytics - Other application data Backup files stored in Google Drive AppData are private and are not publicly accessible. Advertising Mad Trader may display advertisements using Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect certain information, including: - Device identifiers - Advertising identifiers - App usage information Google's use of information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Crash Reporting and Diagnostics Mad Trader may use Firebase Crashlytics or similar services to collect anonymous crash reports and diagnostic information. This information helps improve app stability, reliability, and performance. Data Sharing We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Information may only be shared when required: - To provide core application functionality - To comply with legal obligations - To protect the security and integrity of the application Data Security We take reasonable measures to protect user information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Data Retention User data remains stored on the device unless deleted by the user. Cloud backup data remains stored in the user's Google Drive account until removed by the user. Children's Privacy Mad Trader is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
